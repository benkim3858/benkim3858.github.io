Project Overview

Chordly is a next-generation social media app combining Instagram's intuitive user experience with specialized music matching features for professional musicians and music industry professionals. It provides essential networking and opportunities for music activities including ensemble partners, professional tutors, and paid session musician matching, maximizing collaboration efficiency.

Key Features

Professional Matching System

  • Ensemble/Lesson/Session Matching: Detailed filtering by genre, instrument, and skill level
  • Professional Profiles: Career-focused profiles with instruments and portfolio highlights

Social Networking

  • Instagram-style Feed: Share photos, videos, and audio clips
  • Direct Messages (DM): Real-time 1:1 communication and matching coordination
